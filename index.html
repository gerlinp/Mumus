About Us

Mumu’s Pikliz was founded by Samantha Benoit. As a Haitian American, educator and her passion of cooking and trying different types of food, she founded this business to introduce the world to Haitian cuisine culture.

Her mother taught her how to cook traditional meals with specific spices and herbs and is also the only person who calls Samantha “Mumu” (pronounced: mōō - mōō) and thus “Mumu’s Pikliz” was made!

Mumu’s Pikliz is a food service that provides cooking products that will enhance the flavors of your dishes. The original product was pikliz. Which is a spicy slaw used in just about everything in the Haitian culture. It was immediately suggested that she sell the marinate we put in just about everything as well! My final product was inspired by a customer who made a special request for spicy corn, and it just had to make the menu! Imagine making your famous dish but with enhanced flavors and/or spice! Mumu’s pikliz is designed to do just that!

“My goal is not to change the way you cook, but enhance it!” - Samantha Benoit
